About Me

Greetings, my name is Kamron Dillon and I’m a returning student after 10+ years of absence. The reason I dropped out of college was the feeling of disconnection between what I was being taught and what real world skills I wanted. I was flustered by the education requirements of the profession I wanted to enter. It was like being forced to buy something you don’t want, just to get what you do want. It’s amazing how a decade can change your perception of the world. I used to fear classes like this one, because I would come in with near zero knowledge on the subject. Now instead of fear I feel a sense of adventure.
